    What is Hokm?

    A card game variation based on Persian origin, Hokm is a trick-taking game popular in the middle east. The game uses a standard 52-card deck, and is played by 4 players in teams of 2. Teams sit crosswise.

    The dealer is known as the Trump-caller, and announces the Trump suit of their choosing. The Trump caller then leads the trick, and other players counter with larger valued cards of the same suit. The winner of each trick leads the next.

    Rules:

    The game is played with a full standard deck of 52 cards by four players in fixed partnerships, sitting crosswise.

    Hâkem leads to the first trick. Each player in turn must follow suit. If a player holds no cards of the suit led, that player may play any card (including any Trump card). The highest card in the Trump suit always wins the trick. If no Trump cards are played, the trick goes to the highest card of the suit led. The winner of a trick leads to the next.

    Kot:

    Winning the hand by taking all the first seven tricks is called Kot which is worth 2 points.in other word If the Hâkem’s team wins the hand by taking the first 7 tricks, the other team having taken none, they win 2 points instead of 1.

    Hâkem Koti:

    If you or your team could Kot the Hâkem player or team (impossible mission) then it's called Hâkem Koti which is worth 3 points or If the Hâkem’s opponents win by taking the first 7 tricks, they win 3 points instead of 1.

    Rafee koti:

    Playing a high card to the first trick in an attempt to prevent the opponents from scoring a kot.

    Hokm Lâzem:

    To lead a Trump card which obliges other players to play a Trump card.

    Boridan:

    (literally 'to cut') To win a trick by playing a Trump card when you are void of the suit led.

    Rad Kardan:

    (literally 'to bypass') Not to play a Trump card when you are void of the suit led, because you are quite sure that your partner will win the trick.

    Pâyin amadan

    (literally 'to lay low') To play a low card because you are quite sure that your opponent will win the trick.

    Bâlâ amadan

    (literally 'to lay high') To play a high card in order to make the next player play a higher card if he can.

    Scoring:

    The first team to take 7 tricks wins the hand and scores 1 point.

    The first team to reach 7 points wins the game.

    Joker:

    The Joker originated in the United States during the Civil War and was created as a Trump card for the game of Euchre. It has since been adopted into many other card games, where it often acts as a wild card, but may have other functions such as the top Trump, a skip card (forcing another player to miss a turn).
